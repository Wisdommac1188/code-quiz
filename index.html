Some Rules for this Quiz
1. This is a timed quiz.
2. You will have a total of 75 seconds to answer all the questions.
3. If you answer wrong, the time it took to answer the question will be deducted from your total time left.
4. Once you select your answer you cannot reselect.
5. At the end of the quiz you can submit your initials and highscore to compare to your peers.
